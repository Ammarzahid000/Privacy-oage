Text Genie Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

At Text Genie, we value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

Text Genie is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We temporarily process text selections you explicitly choose to rewrite
  • We maintain anonymous usage counts to enforce weekly limits (5 requests per user)
  • We generate an anonymous user identifier to track usage limits fairly across all users

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or website content beyond your selected text
  • Location data or device information
  • Financial or payment information

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we process is used exclusively to:

  • Provide text rewriting services through Google's Gemini AI API
  • Enforce fair usage limits across our user base
  • Maintain and improve our service quality

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Selected text is sent to Google's Gemini API solely for processing your rewriting requests
  • Anonymous usage data is stored in Firebase to track request limits

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Retention

We practice minimal data retention:

  • Your selected text is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers
  • Anonymous usage counts are reset weekly and only maintained for limit enforcement
  • We do not maintain historical records of your rewritten content

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your information:

  • You can stop using the extension at any time
  • Since we don't store personally identifiable information, deletion requests aren't necessary
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove all local data

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the limited information we process:

  • All communications with Google's API are encrypted
  • Our Firebase database is secured with access controls
  • We regularly review our security practices

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and through the Chrome Web Store listing.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:
