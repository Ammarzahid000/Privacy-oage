Text Genie Privacy Policy

Text Genie does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

The extension only processes text selections temporarily to provide rewriting services through Google's Gemini AI API. Request counts are maintained to enforce weekly limits but are not tied to individual users.

We do not collect: names, emails, locations, browsing history, or any personally identifiable information.

All text processing happens in real-time and is not stored on our servers.
